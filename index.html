A little bit about me.

My journey into tech started with selling online on major platforms like eBay and Amazon. As I worked around on those platforms with certain programs to make easier the selling process, then i came across with HTML codes, which helped me develope things on my own, and I was fascinated with how easily I could develope things. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to take a designer's PSD and convert it into clean, elegant, semantic HTML5 code, and fast to load up to date CSS.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
